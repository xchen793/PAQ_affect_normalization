Stage 2 — Same/Different Speed Task

You will judge whether two faces have the same expression or different expressions. Respond as quickly and accurately as possible. The task is timed.

Your goal

  • Two faces appear side by side (Left / Right).
  • Decide if their expressions are same or different.
  • Respond quickly—your reaction time (RT) is recorded.
  • You cannot go back and only have one chance to finish.
  • Trials advance automatically.

Keys / Responses

Y = Yes (Same) N = No (Different) Space = Start / Continue

What you will see

One face on the left.

Another face on the right.
