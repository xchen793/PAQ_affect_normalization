Instructions

What is this task?

You will judge subtle changes in facial expressions. In the pairwise comparison task, two faces briefly appear side by side, and you decide whether their expressions look the same or different as quickly as possible. In the expression matching task, you will use a slider to adjust the test face from neutral to fully expressive, stopping at the point where it first matches the reference face. This task is untimed.

Use a laptop/desktop at 100% zoom. Sit ~50–70cm from the screen. Avoid glare.
