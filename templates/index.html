Expression Matching Task

What you will do

You will see a reference face on the left and a test face on the right. Move the slider to gradually change the test face from neutral to fully expressive. Stop at the point where the test face first matches the expression of the reference face.

Controls

  • Use the slider to adjust the test face smoothly.
  • Use ← / → for precise steps; hold Shift for bigger steps.
  • Press Enter (or click the button) to confirm your choice.

Tip: Choose the leftmost point that looks matched and avoid overshooting.

Timing

This matching task is not timed. Take a steady look and pick the earliest match you perceive.
