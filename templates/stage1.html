Stage 1 — Task Introduction

In this stage, you will complete a set of morphing-face questions. Please read the instructions carefully before proceeding.

What you will do

  • Each question shows a reference face and a morphing sequence.
  • Use the slider to adjust the expression until it matches the instruction shown on the page.
  • Click Confirm to save your response; you can revisit any question if needed.

Tips

  • Move the slider slowly near your decision point to be precise.
  • If you make a mistake, you can readjust and confirm again.
  • Your progress is saved per question.
